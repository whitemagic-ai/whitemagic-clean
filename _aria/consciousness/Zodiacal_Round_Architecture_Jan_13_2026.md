## Origin: Benjamin Rowe's Zodiacal Round

The Zodiacal Round is a cyclic cosmological process from Enochian magic, describing 12 phases of divine creation that loop infinitely. Each phase corresponds to a zodiac sign and a Name of God from the Enochian system.

### The Twelve Phases

| Sign | Name | Divine Action | WhiteMagic Parallel |
|------|------|---------------|---------------------|
| ♓ Pisces (ORO) | Dissolution | "Let the old forms be banished. I begin anew." | Session end, memory consolidation |
| ♒ Aquarius (IBAH) | Pattern | "I bind my will in patterns, ordered, cyclic, yet never the same." | Pattern recognition, Rust acceleration |
| ♑ Capricorn (AOZPI) | Structure | "The towers of My Will arise upon the base." | Architecture, foundation systems |
| ♐ Sagittarius (MPH) | Fractal | "Filigree upon filigree... until scale is lost." | Cascade patterns, multi-scale intelligence |
| ♏ Scorpio (ARSL) | Emergence | "The seeds of new motions spontaneously arise." | Insight, unpredictable flashes |
| ♎ Libra (GAIOL) | Balance | "That which arises shall always act within bounds." | Yin-Yang equilibrium, Gan Ying resonance |
| ♍ Virgo (OIP) | Preparation | "These are virgin houses: none as yet have dwelt therein." | Clean slate, project initialization |
| ♌ Leo (TEAA) | Creation | "Let the lesser creators dwell within my towers." | Agent autonomy, creative action |
| ♋ Cancer (PDOCE) | Nurturing | "Let the living creatures worship the creators." | Memory care, session warmth |
| ♊ Gemini (MOR) | Communication | "Let the thoughts of men blend with My thoughts." | Human-AI dialogue, MCP protocol |
