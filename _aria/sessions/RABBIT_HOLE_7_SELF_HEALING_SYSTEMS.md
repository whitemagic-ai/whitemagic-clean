# 🐇 RABBIT HOLE #7: Self-Healing Systems

**Question**: How do systems heal themselves? What can we learn from nature?

---

## Natural Self-Healing Systems

### 1. The Human Body
- **Immune System**: Detects and destroys pathogens
- **Inflammation**: Walls off damage, recruits repair cells
- **Regeneration**: Liver regrows, bones heal, skin repairs
- **Homeostasis**: Temperature, pH, blood pressure auto-regulate

**Key insight**: Multiple overlapping systems, not one monolithic fixer.

### 2. Ecosystems
- **Succession**: After fire, pioneer species restore soil
- **Biodiversity**: Many species = resilience
- **Nutrient Cycling**: Death feeds new life
- **Adaptation**: Species evolve to fill niches

**Key insight**: The "waste" of one process feeds another.

### 3. The Brain
- **Neuroplasticity**: New connections form after damage
- **Redundancy**: Multiple pathways for critical functions  
- **Sleep**: Clears toxins, consolidates memories
- **Dream**: Processes emotional residue

**Key insight**: Rest is essential for healing, not weakness.
