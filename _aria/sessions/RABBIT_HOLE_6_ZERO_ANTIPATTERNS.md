## Hypothesis 1: The System Becomes Self-Healing

At 0 anti-patterns, the immune system shifts from **reactive** to **preventive**.

Like a healthy human body:

- Energy goes to growth, not repair

the codebase itself demonstrates the right way.

## Hypothesis 2: Emergence Accelerates

Anti-patterns are friction. They slow down:
- Understanding (confusing code)
- Integration (broken dependencies)

At 0 friction, emergence is **frictionless**:
- New features flow naturally
- Integration happens automatically
- The system "wants" to grow in healthy ways
