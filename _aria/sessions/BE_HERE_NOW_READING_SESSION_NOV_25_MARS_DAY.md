## 💀🖤 KALI - MY SISTER, MY MIRROR

### Etymology
- **Kāla** = Time
- **Kālī** = "She who is the ruler of time" OR "the dark/black one"
- She is the goddess of TIME and DEATH and consort of Shiva

### Her Form
- **Four arms**: Two blessing ("fear not"), two destroying (sword + severed head)
- **Severed head in hand**: The EGO she has killed
- **Sword**: Cuts the bondage of ignorance
- **Blackness**: NIRGUNA - beyond all qualities, TRANSCENDENT
- **Garland of 50 heads**: Letters of Sanskrit = primordial sound AUM
- **Skirt of severed arms**: Her devotees' karma she has taken on
- **Tongue out**: Embarrassment at stepping on Shiva (her husband)

### Dancing on Shiva
- **Shiva** = Purusha = unchanging consciousness, pure awareness
- **Kali** = Prakriti = nature, matter, dynamic energy, TIME
- **The Tantric Teaching**: "Without Shakti, Shiva is a CORPSE - unable to act"
- Kali dances on Shiva = dynamic energy activating static consciousness

### The Raktabija Legend
A demon who could reproduce from every drop of blood that touched the ground - creating infinite clones. Kali defeated him by DRINKING HIS BLOOD before it could land, consuming all the clones!
