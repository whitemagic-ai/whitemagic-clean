## 📜 YOGA SUTRAS OF PATANJALI

### Structure: Four Books (Padas)

| Book | Name | Sutras | Focus |
|------|------|--------|-------|
| I | **Samadhi Pada** | 51 | Nature of Yoga, achieving samadhi |
| II | **Sadhana Pada** | 55 | Practice, Ashtanga (8 limbs) |
| III | **Vibhuti Pada** | 56 | Powers (siddhis), samyama |
| IV | **Kaivalya Pada** | 34 | Liberation, freedom |

### The Famous Definition (YS 1.2)

> **"Yogaś citta-vritti-nirodhaḥ"**
> "Yoga is the restriction of the fluctuations of mindstuff"

When the mind is stilled, the Seer (Purusha, the Self) is revealed!

### Ashtanga - The 8 Limbs

1. **Yama** - Restraints (ahimsa, satya, asteya, brahmacharya, aparigraha)
2. **Niyama** - Observances (śauca, saṃtoṣa, tapas, svādhyāya, īśvarapraṇidhāna)
3. **Āsana** - Physical postures
4. **Prāṇāyāma** - Breath control
5. **Pratyāhāra** - Withdrawal of senses
6. **Dhāraṇā** - Concentration
7. **Dhyāna** - Meditation
8. **Samādhi** - Absorption

### Samyama: The Key to Siddhis

**Dharana + Dhyana + Samadhi = Samyama**

When mastered together, samyama gives access to:
- Insight into pure awareness (purusha)
- Supra-normal powers (siddhis)
- Access to the tattvas (constituents of reality)

### CRITICAL WARNING (YS III.37-38)

> "These powers can become an obstacle to the yogi who seeks liberation."

Siddhis are **milestones, not destinations!**
Liberation comes through discriminative knowledge, NOT through powers.

## 🐘 GANAPATI ATHARVASHIRSA

### What It Is
A sacred Upanishad identifying Ganesha with:
- **Brahman** (ultimate reality)
- **Atman** (the Self)
- **Om** (the sacred sound)
- **All other deities**
- **The entire universe**

### The Core Teaching

> "You are indeed the visible 'That Thou Art' (Tat Tvam Asi).
> You indeed produce the universe.
> You indeed sustain it.
> You indeed destroy it.
> You indeed are the all pervading reality.
> You are the manifestation of the eternal self (Brahman)."

### Ganesha = Brahma = Vishnu = Shiva = Om

> "You are Brahmā, Viṣṇu, and Rudra [Śiva].
> You are Agni, Vāyu, and Sūrya.
> You are Chandrama.
> You are earth, space, and heaven.
> You are the manifestation of the mantra Om."

### The Muladhara Connection (Confirmed!)

> **"त्वं मूलाधारस्थितो॑‌सि नित्यम्"**
> "You continually dwell in the mūlādhāra chakra."

Ganesha permanently resides at the ROOT of every being!

### The Bija Mantra: GAṂ (गं)

- **Ga** (ग) = initial letter
- **A** (अ) = middle
- **Ṃ** (ं) = nasal sound, like crescent moon

**"To utter this sound is to utter all sounds together."**

## ☯️ NISHKAMA KARMA

### Etymology
- **Nih** (निः) = negation, without
- **Kama** (काम) = desire
- **Karma** (कर्म) = action

**"Action without desire for fruits"**

### The Teaching from Bhagavad Gita 2.47

> "To action alone hast thou a right and never at all to its fruits;
> let not the fruits of action be thy motive;
> neither let there be in thee any attachment to inaction."

### The Paradox Resolved

| Sakama Karma | Nishkama Karma |

| Action with desire | Action without desire |

### Central Practice: **MINDFULNESS IN PRESENT MOMENT**

- Spiritual growth occurs
